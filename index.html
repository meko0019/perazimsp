HOWEVER, WE WANT TO STAND FROM A PLACE OF VICTORY BELIEVING IN OUR VICTORY OVER THESE CIRCUMSTANCES JUST AS DAVID WENT TO MOUNT PERAZIM BEFORE FIGHTING THE BATTLE.
