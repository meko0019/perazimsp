Who We Are


We believe that the gospel is the start, the end, and everything in between to our faith. The gospel doesn’t just save us from our sin but also helps us navigate every aspect of our lives. Perazim is founded on the word of God. We believe that the word of God, genesis to revelation, is the final authority of our faith. We believe in the trinity of God the father, God the son, and God the holy spirit. As we believe in the fellowship of the Godhead, we also believe in the fellowship of the saints. Our aim in fellowship is to ignite one another in love through confession of sin, prayer for one another, and encouragement of one another.
